You must hone your awareness like the tip of the knife.

It is like a seed dropped in soil. You can determine its environment, but not when it sprouts.

Never forget Jerusalem.

Error, like a disease has spread because you don't know who you are or where you came from. Remember. This is your task upon the Earth.

Don't listen to the wiles of Error, don't let it lead you astray. Set your mind on Truth, which stands crowned and conquering, never faltering.

The Good News of Truth

(Nag Hammadi Codex I and XII)

Translated by Josiah

Ignorance of Reality brings about Error

The ignorance of the Truth brought about anguish and fear. The fear grew thick like a fog, so that all became unable to see. Because of this, Error was strengthened. In vanity and ignorance, it worked on its own matter, preparing a substitute for the Truth.

This was not a disgrace for the Uncontainable, Inconceivable One, because the anguish and forgetfulness and delusion of the deception was like nothing, whereas the Truth stands forever, unchangeable, undisturbed, and ever completely beautiful.

For this reason, disregard Error, since it has no true root.
